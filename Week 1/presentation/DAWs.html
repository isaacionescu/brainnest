What are the most popular DAWs?

A DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) is a piece of software that's used to create music digitally. With the advent of the technological boom and Internet since the 90's/2000's, making music in your own bedroom has become increasingly accessible to all people around the world — a lot of times, for free!

Some of the most used DAWs include:

  • Ableton Live 11
  • Image Line FL Studio 21
  • Logic Pro (Mac only)
  • Reason
  • Cubase
  • Pro Tools (for bands)
